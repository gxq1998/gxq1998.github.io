330 Cedar St, Room LMP108

New Haven, CT 06511

I am a fourth-year PhD candidate at the School of Engineering and Applied Science, Yale University, where I am fortunately co-advised by Prof. Chi Liu at Yale Positron Emission Tomography (PET) Center and Prof. Nicha Dvornek at Yale Image Processing and Analysis Group. Before coming to Yale, I did my undergraduate study at Shanghai Jiao Tong University fortunately supervised by Prof. Jun Zhao. In summer 2022, I was an image analytics research intern at Siemens Healthineers.

My research interests are medical image analysis and medical computer vision, specializing in but not limited to registration, denoising, and segmentation for dynamic PET, CT, and MRI.

I am actively looking for summer internship positions in summer 2023. Please feel free to contact me at xueqi.guo@yale.edu and find my CV here.
